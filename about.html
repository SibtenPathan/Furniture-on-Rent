About Us

Furniture is a leading Furniture and Appliances Rental Brand in India. With a team of experienced artisans, the brand creates beautiful and durable pieces that blend traditional and modern styles. Our furniture is inspired by the Indian urban user and how they live in their homes.

Our History

Institute of Technology brings together diverse skills and a shared enthusiasm for technology. Since our inception, we have grown from a small group with big ideas to a cohesive unit dedicated to learning, creating, and making a positive impact in the field of Computer Science and Technology. Our journey is marked by collaboration, exploration, and a commitment to pushing the boundaries of what's possible in tech education.
